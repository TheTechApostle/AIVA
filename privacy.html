Privacy Policy

How ARIA collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our voice scheduling assistant.

Last updated: April 2026
01

Overview

ARIA ("we", "our", "the app") is a voice-powered AI scheduling assistant that integrates with Google Calendar. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

By using ARIA at https://aiva-1-mxgo.onrender.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

✅ We do not sell your data, run ads, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.
02

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the scheduling service:

  • Google Calendar data — event titles, times, descriptions, and attendees, accessed only when you connect your account and make a request
  • Voice audio — microphone recordings you submit for transcription, processed immediately and not stored
  • Chat messages — your text and voice messages to ARIA, held in temporary session memory only
  • Google OAuth tokens — access and refresh tokens stored in server memory to maintain your calendar connection during a session
03

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide the app's core features:

  • Reading your Google Calendar to answer scheduling questions
  • Creating, updating, and deleting calendar events on your behalf
  • Transcribing your voice messages using OpenAI Whisper
  • Generating AI responses using OpenAI GPT-4o

We do not use your data to train AI models, build advertising profiles, or for any purpose beyond responding to your direct requests.

04

Third-Party Services

ARIA uses the following third-party services to function. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Calendar API — to read and manage your calendar events (Google Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI API — to power AI chat responses and voice transcription (OpenAI Privacy Policy)
  • Render — cloud hosting for the application (Render Privacy Policy)
05

Data Retention

ARIA is designed with minimal data retention:

  • Chat history and OAuth tokens are stored in server memory only and are erased when the server restarts
  • Voice audio is transcribed immediately and never saved to disk permanently
  • We do not use a database — no personal data is written to persistent storage
🔒 Closing your browser or server restart clears all session data. Nothing about you is stored long-term.
06

Google OAuth & Calendar Access

When you connect Google Calendar, you grant ARIA permission to read and manage your calendar events. Specifically:

  • We request https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar scope
  • Access is limited to your primary calendar
  • You can revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • We never access your Gmail, contacts, Drive, or any other Google service
07

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access — you can ask us what data we hold about you at any time
  • Deletion — disconnect Google Calendar and close your session to remove all data
  • Revocation — revoke Google access at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Portability — your calendar data belongs to you and lives in Google Calendar
08

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS
  • OAuth tokens are never logged or written to files
  • API keys are stored as environment variables, not in source code
  • The app is hosted on Render with automatic security updates
09

Children's Privacy

ARIA is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at https://aiva-1-mxgo.onrender.com/privacy with an updated date. Continued use of ARIA after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, we're happy to help.
